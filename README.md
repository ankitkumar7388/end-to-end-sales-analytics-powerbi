# end-to-end-sales-analytics-powerbi
# Company Background :
AtliQ Hardware is a leading supplier of computer hardware and peripherals to major retail clients (Surge Stores, Nomad Stores, Excel Stores, and Electricalsara Stores) across India, with its head office located in Delhi.

# The Business Problem : 
Despite operating in a dynamically growing market, AtliQ Hardware is experiencing a decline in overall sales. Sales Director Bhavin Patel lacks actionable data to track regional performance, making it difficult to identify which specific markets are succeeding and which are underperforming.
